Wannabe Dahl

Description

A very easy to make dahl that is not very authentic but definitely tastes good

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Red lentils
  • Coconut cream
  • Tomato paste
  • Fresh tomate
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Sour cream
  • Fresh parsley leaves

Recipe

  1. Chop onion and garlic, fry on low heat until getting golden
  2. Add red lentils and fry for a little bit
  3. Add coconut cream and tomato sauce
  4. Chop tomatoes and add to dahl
  5. Let it simmer on low heat till lentils are soft (30+mins)
  6. Serve with rice and top with sour cream and fresh parsley leaves
  7. Optional: buy frozen prata in supermarket, fry and serve with dahl
  8. Enjoy!
