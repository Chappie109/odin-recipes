Spinach Tofu Pasta

Description

Vegie pasta dish packed with protein, veggies and good flavours

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Spinach
  • Tofu
  • Cream cheese / Ricotta
  • Brocolli
  • Zuchinni
  • Pine nuts
  • Parmesan / feta cheese
  • Garlic

Recipe

  1. Cut Brocolli and zuchinni into pieces, roast in the oven until cripsy
  2. Blend spinach with garlic, tofu, cream cheese and spices until smooth
  3. Boil pasta
  4. Roast pine nuts
  5. Add blended sauce to pasta, add roasted veggies, stir through raw spinach leaves and topple with pine nuts and feta cheese
  6. Enjoy!
