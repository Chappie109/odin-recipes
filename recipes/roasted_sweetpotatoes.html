Roasted sweet potatoes

Description

This recipe will help you make the yummiest loaded sweet potatoes. Low effort, happy wallet and satisfied belly guaranteed!

Ingredients

  • Sweet potatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Sour cream (yoghurt for healthy option)
  • Kale

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven at 180 degrees
  2. Wash the sweet potato, poke some holes with a fork all around the skin. Put the sweet potatato in the oven for 40mins or until soft and tender
  3. Rince chickpeas, put on oven plate and cover with olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika and chili powder. Roast chickpeas for 30mins in same oven
  4. Rince kale, sprinkle with olive oil and salt, roast for 10mins
  5. When sweet potato is soft and chickpeas crispy, get out of the oven. Cut open sweet potato and load with chickpeas, kale and top with sour cream
